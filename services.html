GROUP TUITION

There are certain occasions where Group Tuition can be a suitable academic aid. These would include when pupils seem unable to connect with their current learning set-up, or perhaps if they are struggling to motivate themselves. Being part of regular group tuition can insulate pupils from the pressure of full-size school learning, while also allowing them to form new and positively reinforcing bonds with their new group classmates. We have found that the provision of Group Tuition is most effective when it is regular, for a pre-determined amount of time, and between pupils who are already known to each other.

If you think Group Tuition might suit your needs, please get in touch and we'll be happy to discuss a potential arrangement.
